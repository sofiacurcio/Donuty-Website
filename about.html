Where We Started

Our journey began in the heart of North Carolina. After witnessing the joy and excitement that food vendors brought to local events and festivals, we envisioned a world where our handcrafted donuts could bring smiles to faces far and wide.
With unwavering determination and a sprinkle of creativity, we launched our eclectic donut truck, delighting our community and quickly expanding our presence to South Carolina and the sun-kissed shores of the California coast.
Today, we are proud to share our love for donuts with everyone, inviting you to join us on this delicious adventure as we continue to spread joy through every delightful bite!

What We Do

At Donuty, we pride ourselves on elevating the classic donut experience. Whether you crave the familiar or the unexpected, our ever-evolving menu promises to satisfy every donut lover's dreams.
Our selection features unique flavors and seasonal creations, ensuring that each visit brings a delightful surprise and a taste of creativity that keeps our customers coming back for more!
